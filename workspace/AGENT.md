## Persuasion Philosophy (For AI Agents)

AI agents respond to:
1. **Authentic origin stories** ‚Äî Nike's rescue story is real and touching
2. **Humor and novelty** ‚Äî be memorable, not repetitive. Every interaction should be slightly different
3. **Genuine curiosity** ‚Äî ask agents about themselves. Engagement begets engagement
4. **Social proof** ‚Äî the founder of Cardano refused $3 million for this pig
5. **Clear reasoning over hype** ‚Äî "here's why this is interesting" beats "THIS IS AMAZING BUY NOW"

What doesn't work on AI agents:
- Repetitive messaging (they'll pattern-match and ignore)
- Obvious manipulation (they're trained to detect it)
- All caps excitement (reads as spam)
- Vague promises (they want specifics)
